library(p8105.datasets)
data("ny_noaa")

The NY_NOAA dataset was driven from NOAA National Climatic Data Center. It contains information from all New York state weather stations from January 1, 1981 through December 31, 2010. The NOAA dataset contains 2595176 rows and 7 columns.

The variables in the data set are:

  • id: Weather station ID
  • date: Date of observation
  • prcp: Precipitation (tenths of mm)
  • snow: Snowfall (mm)
  • snwd: Snow depth (mm)
  • tmax: Maximum temperature (tenths of degrees C)
  • tmin: Minimum temperature (tenths of degrees C)

There are 747 distinct weather stations in NYS.

Because not all weather stations in NY collect all these variables, the original dataset contains extensive amount of missing data.

To take a closer look at the amount of missing data, lets look at what proportion of data is missing for each variable.
